FinSight

FinSight is a Streamlit-based personal finance dashboard that helps users track income, expenses, and monthly spending patterns. It features CRUD operations on transactions, interactive charts, and a modern dark-mode UI, built using Pandas, NumPy, and Altair, and deployed on Streamlit Cloud.

Traverse

Traverse is an AI-powered travel platform that enhances safety, accessibility, and decision-making for travelers. It combines smart route recommendations, crowd and risk prediction, accessibility-aware navigation, AR-based cultural exploration, offline translation, and budget planning into a single unified system, promoting safer, inclusive, and responsible travel.

Conference Papers

SeaTorch: AI-Driven Deep Learning Model for Migration Pattern Prediction With Bilingual Forecasting Support for Sustainable Fisheries

This paper proposes an accessible digital platform aimed at supporting traditional fishermen by addressing challenges such as unpredictable weather, declining fish stocks, language barriers, and limited access to modern tools. The study presents a predictive, feature-driven approach focused on bilingual support, low-literacy usability, and community-centered design. By integrating planned features such as fish location insights, weather alerts, marine animal awareness, and legal guidance, the paper highlights the potential of human-centered digital solutions to improve safety, livelihoods, and sustainability within coastal fishing communities.
